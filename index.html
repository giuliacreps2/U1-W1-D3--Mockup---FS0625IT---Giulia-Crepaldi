Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Suace

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water (110° to 115°)
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, plus more for brushing

For the topping:

  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen cranberries
  • 4 ounces Brie cheese, rind removed and cut into small pieces
  • Fresh rosemary sprigs (optional)

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
